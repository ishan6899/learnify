About Us
 Why Us

Learnify is an institute for engineering entrance examination where we focus to develop the analytical thinking of students through innovative and motivating methods. Here we focus on developing the thinking of the students over various concept of Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics through physical class room teaching, assignments and tests.

Our Mission

To help rediscover the wonder of learning and kindling a passion for genuine understanding of the subject.

Our Popular Courses
 The Best Courses

Genisis

Genesis is a three year foundation program for class X students.This program aims to build a solid base for preparation of IITJEE, while keeping things interesting.

Ascension

Ascension is a two year program for class XI students which starts at a comfortable pace to allow students to cope a sudden change and picks up the required pace.

Zenith

Zenith is a one year program for class XII students. This course takes along class XI revision with class XII course. It also includes finishing touches with last mile test series.
